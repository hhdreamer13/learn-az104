Self-service password reset (SSPR)

SSPR lets users reset their own forgotten or expired password — in a browser or at the Windows sign-in screen — without calling the help desk. You choose which authentication methods prove their identity, who it applies to, and (for hybrid) whether the new password flows back to on-premises AD.
