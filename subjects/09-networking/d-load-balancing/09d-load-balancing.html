Load balancing

Distribute traffic across multiple backends for performance and high availability. The choice turns on which OSI layer and regional vs global: Load Balancer (L4, TCP/UDP) and Application Gateway (L7, HTTP/S, WAF) are the two AZ-104 stars — with Front Door and Traffic Manager as the global options.
